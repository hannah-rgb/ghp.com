HANNAH
PARK
Grace Hannah Park navigates the spaces between
type, motion, print, and code to question how medium shapes message.
Her work often begins with noticing how a moving image can
breathe new life into a static poster, or how certain ideas only make sense
once printed, folded, or bound. Lately, she’s been drawn to the tactile intelligence of street posters—their materiality, their presence—
and how these qualities might be translated into digital formats. With
a practice grounded in both formal design and experimental tools, she treats graphic design as a responsive system rather than a fixed outcome.
